About Us

The Beverly Hills Master Association is dedicated to fostering leadership, discipline, and excellence through mentorship, Taekwondo philosophy, and global engagement.

GrandMaster Kim

7th Dan Taekwondo • Olympic Coach • Global Educator

GrandMaster Kim is an internationally respected martial artist, Olympic head coach, educator, and leader who has shaped the lives of thousands of students worldwide.

His mission is to instill the five core tenets of Taekwondo—Courtesy, Integrity, Perseverance, Self-Control, and Indomitable Spirit—while creating accessible pathways for all students to train, grow, and thrive.

Highlights

  • Head Coach – Kazakhstan National Team (2004 Athens Olympics)
  • President – Beverly Hills BMA
  • Kukkiwon (World Taekwondo HQ) Board Member
  • 7th Dan Kukkiwon Black Belt
  • 4th Dan Kumdo / 1st Dan Judo
  • Instructor in Korea, Frankfurt, Vancouver & Shanghai
  • Taught Taekwondo to Mayor & City Council of Beverly Hills

Education

  • Kyunghee University – BA (Taekwondo)
  • Yonsei University – BS
  • Korea University – MA & Ph.D

Mission Statement

GrandMaster Kim believes Taekwondo is more than a martial art — it is a lifelong journey of discipline, leadership, and personal growth.

His passion is to help students of all backgrounds gain confidence, respect, teamwork, and the mindset required to become future leaders.

Through global teaching experiences in Korea, Germany, Kazakhstan, Canada, and China, he empowers students to embrace cultural understanding, community service, and a strong moral foundation.
